Mac and Cheese

Charli XCX has shared a general approach to making Mac and Cheese, though specifics might vary based on her exact preferences. Here’s a simplified version of a typical Mac and Cheese recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 225g (8 oz) elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • Bread crumbs (optional, for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Macaroni:

    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni and cook until al dente, according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the Roux:

    In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the flour and whisk constantly for about 2 minutes, until the mixture is golden and bubbly.

  3. Add the Milk:

    Gradually add the milk to the roux, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Continue to cook and whisk until the mixture thickens, about 3-5 minutes.

  4. Add the Cheese:

    Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the shredded cheddar and mozzarella cheeses until completely melted and smooth. Season with salt, black pepper, and paprika, if using.

  5. Combine with Macaroni:

    Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir until well combined and the macaroni is evenly coated with the sauce.

  6. Optional Baking Step:

    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Transfer the mac and cheese to a baking dish and top with bread crumbs. Bake for about 20 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.

  7. Serve:

    Serve the mac and cheese hot, straight from the saucepan or baking dish. Enjoy the creamy, cheesy goodness!

Enjoy your homemade Mac and Cheese, a delicious and comforting dish that reflects Charli XCX’s love for classic comfort foods!
